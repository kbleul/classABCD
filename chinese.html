Phrases & Sentences 

In Hanzi/汉子

Meaning

Nihao

你好

Hello

Ninhao

Hello (for older or respected folk)

Nimen hao

Hello (to multiple people)

Nihao ma?

你好吗？

How are you?

Wo hen hao

I am fine

Dialogue

Example 1 : A: 你好！Hello!

B：你好！Hello!

A：你好吗？ How are you? 

B: 我很好，谢谢。你呢？ I am fine, thank you. And you?

A： 我也很好。I am fine too. 

Example 2: A：早上好！

B: 早上好，你们吗？

A：我很好。

B:好。 再见。

Some Dialogue Examples

Example 1: A: 你经常踢足球吗？ Do you often play football?

B: 不经常。我喜欢经常爬山。Not often. I often prefer mountain climbing.

Example 2: A: 你喜欢 哪种颜色？ Which color do you like?

B: 我 喜欢 蓝色的。你呢？ I like the blue one. What about you?

A：我更喜欢红色的。I prefer the red one.

Example 3: A： 这个桌子多少钱？How much is this table?

B: 是 2000 RMB。 It is 2000 RMB. 

A： 太贵了。便宜一点儿吧。That’s quite expensive. Lower the price a bit. 

Example 4: A： 火车站的队伍很长。The line at the railway station is so long.

B: 对。我们叫辆出租车吧。 Yes. Let’s call a taxi.

Some Dialogue Examples

Example 1: A: 餐厅暂时关闭。 The restaurant is temporarily closed.

B: 不好了。我会带我女朋友去那里吃午饭？ Oh no. Where will I take my girlfriend out for lunch now?

A: 你们可以去附近的新面馆。You can go to the new noodle restaurant nearby.

Example 2: A: 她的皮肤看起来很年轻。Her skin looks so young. 

B：我注意到。就她的年龄而言，她看起来很年轻。 I noticed. She looks so young for her age. 

Example 3: A: 我今天很生气。 I’m very angry today. 

B： 你为什么生气？Why are you angry?

A： 我姐姐偷偷看了我的日记。 My sister secretly read my diary. 

Example 4: A: 我想我感冒了。我应该怎么办 ？ I think I’m catching a cold. What should I do? 

B: 你应该喝热水，好好休息。 You should drink hot water and rest well.
